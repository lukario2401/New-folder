Lungs

Your lungs are a big part of your breathing system, which includes different body parts that help you breathe. They are used to make oxygen travel through our body. It gives oxygen to blood. While heart is pumping it, it supplies it with oxygen. It it also prevalent in the process of gas exchange. Gas exchange is the process of taking in oxygen and getting rid of carbon dioxide in our bodies. It occurs in the lungs through the diffusion of gases in the tiny air sacs called alveoli.

Nose

When you breathe in, air flows through your nostrils and enters the nasal cavity. Cells in the nasal cavity release mucus. This mucus contains enzymes called lysozymes. They kill bacteria for us. The outside of the nasal cavity has nose hairs that get coated with the mucus. This helps them trap a lot of different slightly harmful particles, etc.
